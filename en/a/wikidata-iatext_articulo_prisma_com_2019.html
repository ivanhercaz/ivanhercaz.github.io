Article about the Wikidata-IATEXT project published on Prisma.com

vie 10 enero 2020

At the end of December it was published the number 40 of Prisma.com, Revista de Ciências e Tecnologias da Informação e Comunicação, in which I have the pleasure to have published an article with Manuel Ramírez Sánchez, coordinator of the Documental Heritage and Bibliometry division of the Research Institute of Text Analysis and Applications (IATEXT), and Gregorio Rodríguez Herrera, director of the IATEXT.

The article, titled Wikidata, WikiCite and Scholia as tools for a corpus of linked bibliographic data. Curation and structuring of the scientific production of the IATEXT’s researchers, consists in a scientific and detailed presentation of the project Wikidata-IATEXT, its motivation and state of the art, the applied methodology, the obtained results, possible use cases, the problems that we address and the conclusions.

The article can be readed in the website of the journal, as well as in accedaCRIS, the institutional repository of the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, where it has assined a Handle System identifier: http://hdl.handle.net/10553/60056. It is also available in ResearchGate. In ResearchGate are available the images used, however in accedaCRIS I forgot to add them when I upload the article. I will contact with the technical support to know if it is possible add them, if not, the short report I posted in this blog will always remain.

In this number of the journal has also been published the article Q392754: Criação de perfis académicos com Wikidata e Scholia – projecto-piloto, written by two researchers that I have the pleasure of meeting them, Catarina Mateus Reis and Miguel Mimoso Correia. Their article is similar to ours, but this case of study is different in several aspects. I recommend to read it.
