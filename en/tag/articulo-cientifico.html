Artículo sobre el proyecto Wikidata-IATEXT publicado en Prisma.com

At the end of Decemeber it was published the number 40 of Prisma.com, Revista de Ciências e Tecnologias da Informação e Comunicação, in which I have the pleasure to have published an article with Manuel Ramírez Sánchez, coordinator of the division of Documental Heritage and Bibliometry division of the Research Institute of Text Analysis and Applications (IATEXT), and Gregorio Rodríguez Herrera, director of the IATEXT. The article is a scientific and detailed presentation of the Wikidata-IATEXT project.
